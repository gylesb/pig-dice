Let's Play some Pig Dice!

Rules to get you started so you aren't completely lost:

  • Choose to play either against another user or the computer (we'd recommend the user)
  • Click on the corresponding "Roll Dice!" button to roll the virutal die
  • When you roll the dice, a number from 1-6 is displayed which represents what score will be added to the player
  • When a player rolls a "1", then their points are lost for that turn and the turn passes to the other player
  • A player can choose to either roll again and take a chance or hold onto their accumalated points for the turn and have it added to their total score
  • First player to 100 wins!
